Pokémon Deck Checklist Generator

Paste a deck list in standard export format. It auto-detects the Pokémon / Trainer / Energy sections and builds a printable checklist. Counts are calculated from the list.

Format & tips

Each card line is quantity name SET number, e.g. 4 Froakie CRI 20.

A line starting with Pokémon, Trainer, or Energy starts a new section. Optional sub-groups: any other line ending in a colon, e.g. Greninja line: or Supporters: — cards below it sit under that label. Total Cards: lines are ignored.
